Constants

BUFFER_SIZE_DEFAULT

Sample count for calculations. It affects both latency and computation performance. Latency perception for musical instruments: over ~12ms is already disturbing for some players.
PI2

= π x 2
SAMPLE_RATE_DEFAULT

= 192 kHz, as humans can discern time differences of 5 microseconds. Humans can hear < 1° difference in the location of the sound source, when it is in front of them. See also: interaural time difference
TONE_FREQUENCY_MAX

= 28 kHz, the highest hearable (feelable) frequency. Overtones above this frequency are filtered out from calculations. See: hearing range
TONE_FREQUENCY_MIN

= 3 Hz, the lowest feelable frequency. Tones below it will not be calculated. The hearable lowest is 12 Hz. See: hearing range
